Policy Name: Routine Account Support Policy

This policy applies when a customer requests routine account help such as password resets, account balance questions, address updates, phone number changes, debit card replacements, or general online banking support.

If no fraud, compliance, or escalation signals are present, the call can remain in the standard support queue. The agent should verify the customer, complete the requested account service, and confirm the issue is resolved.

Recommended Action: Keep in normal support queue and complete the requested account service.
